What is Carbon Footprinting ?

The time is past when humankind thought it could selfishly draw on exhaustible resources we know now the world is not a commodity any more. Carbon Footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases produced to directly and indirectly support human activities usually expressed in equivalent tons of carbon dioxide. Your carbon footprint is the sum of all emissions of carbon dioxide which were induced by your activities in a given time frame ,expressed in equivalent tons of carbon dioxide. Our carbon footprint is the sum of all emissions of carbon dioxide which were induced by your activities in a given time frame, usually a carbon footprint is calculated for the time period of the year.

Why we need to care ?

The rapidly increased accumulation of greenhouse gases by human activity in our atmosphere is what contributes to global warming. The bigger the carbon footprint of any activity, the greater the cumulative contribution to climate change.every action you take that releases greenhouse gases into the atmosphere. The more you use, the bigger your footprint. A Smaller Carbon Footprint is Good for the planet and your wallet we have a single mission to protect and hand on the planet to the next generation
